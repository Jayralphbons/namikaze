Nuka Notebook: This notebook is fully rewritable and waterproof. The paper is unique and unbreakable, providing a tactile smooth writing experience. You can write, erase, and write again on the same page with any type of pen, pencil, or marker. The notebook components are made of waterproof materials that haven't been used in other stationery before. The notes can't be destroyed even if you spill water or any other drink on it. The next-generation paper won't tear, ensuring the notebook stays new for a long time

Nuka Pencil: This eternal metal pencil has a tip made of a unique metal alloy. It writes like a regular pencil, but you don't need to worry about sharpening or broken leads. The writings can be wiped off with the Nuka magic eraser

Nuka Eraser: This eraser is designed to work with the Nuka Pencil and Notebook, allowing you to erase and rewrite on the same page.
